How to open Navigation Panel and operate the appilation?

Navigation

To open navigation panel, click on "☰" which is at top left corner. Now you can see all the menu and submenus from here.

How to add Product?

Add Product

To add a new product, navigate to "☰" > "Action" > "Add Products". Fill in the required information and click "Submit" and wait a while. A popup messege will appear. To add nore than one product, click on "Add Row".
